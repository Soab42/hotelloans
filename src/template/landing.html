Our Mortgage Products

Hotel Loans Programs

SBA 7(A) or 504 Loans

Our SBA loans include 7(A) or 504 loans tailored to your unique needs, with loan amounts of up to $5 million. We consider factors like loan amount, revenue, occupancy rate, credit and projections to find the right solution for you.

Bridge Loans

When traditional lenders or banks’ guidelines don’t quite fit your financing needs, a Bridge loan can be your ideal solution. Enjoy the flexibility of a 1 year to 3 years term for purchase or refinance with several options.

Construction Loans

Our expertise extends nationwide, offering SBA 7(A), conventional, or private lender loans. With loan amounts ranging from $1 to $100 million, our experienced team will review, and guide you through the approval process.

USDA Loans

USDA B&I loans present an attractive option for real estate acquisition, new construction, or renovations in rural communities. Eligibility, creditworthiness, location, and your business plan play key roles in securing these loans.

Large Project Loans

For ambitious large-scale projects that banks may hesitate to back, our dedicated team steps in to help with loans ranging from $10 million to $100 million. Whether you’re planning to purchase or build, we’ve got you covered.

Permanent Loans

Our experienced team can guide you through the loan application process, helping you structure the loan and complete the initial underwriting to ensure you qualify for a long-term conventional loan with the best interest rates.
